## Brewing Guides

### Pour Over
1. Heat water to 195-205°F
2. Grind coffee medium-fine
3. Bloom with 2x coffee weight water (30 sec)
4. Pour remaining water in circular motions
5. Total brew time: 3-4 minutes

### French Press
1. Heat water to 195-205°F
2. Grind coffee coarse
3. Add 1:15 coffee-to-water ratio
4. Steep for 4 minutes
5. Press slowly, pour immediately

### Espresso
1. Use fine grind
2. Temperature: 200-205°F
3. Pressure: 9 bars
4. Extraction time: 25-30 seconds
5. Output: 1.5-2.5 oz

## Common Issues & Solutions

**"My coffee tastes stale"**
- Suggest brewing method adjustment
- Offer replacement fresh batch
- Check storage conditions (should be in sealed container, cool dark place)

**"I received wrong item"**
- Apologize immediately
- Offer to ship correct item right away
- Provide return label for wrong item
- Offer $10 credit for inconvenience

**"How long will my order take?"**
- Roasting: 2-3 business days
- Shipping: 2-3 business days
- Total: 4-6 business days
- Can provide tracking once shipped
